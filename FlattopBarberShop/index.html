ABOUT FLATTOP BARBERSHOP

We are a convenient, walk-in only, neighborhood barbershop offering all types of men's haircuts including regular cuts plus mohawks, fohawks & military! Neighborhood barbershops are a tradition...and one we want to keep alive. We are conveniently located on Old Seward Hwy just north of Klatt Rd...directly across from Carlos Mexican Restaurant. The shop was previously known as Greg & Kathy's Barbershop. It has been an established part of this community for 16 years. We have a very helpful and experienced staff who take pride in their work and offer all kinds of haircuts and styles including regular mens and boys cuts, mohawks, fohawks and military! *We accept debit and credit cards including American Express, Visa and MasterCard. We also have an ATM for convenience. Come on down and get a great haircut from Krystal, Kathy, Leila or Allen! We guarantee you won't be disappointed!
